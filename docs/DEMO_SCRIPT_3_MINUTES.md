# Three-Minute Demo Script

**Use demo data only. Do not conduct an emergency demonstration.**

## 0:00-0:25 - Framing

“FanSafe is an offline-first safety companion for international travellers.
It is a prototype: it does not contact emergency services, authenticate
tickets, or replace medical care.”

## 0:25-1:05 - Offline and city scope

Show the installed PWA after a cached reload. Select Toronto and point out
the sample-data and source-review status. Explain that 911 is emergency, 311
is city services, and 211 is community referral; neither is shown as police
non-emergency. Switch to New York City and say New Jersey is not covered.

## 1:05-1:45 - Travel support

Open Translate, choose a safety phrase, and point out the native-speaker
review warning. Open a trusted contact and explain that actions prepare or
share a message through the device; FanSafe does not claim delivery.

## 1:45-2:25 - Privacy and medical card

Show the medical card hidden by default, reveal it deliberately, and show
the local-only warning. Do not enter real medical or contact data during a
public demo.

## 2:25-3:00 - Honest close

“The code is ready for inspection, but public alpha is still blocked by
independent review of city data and a denied-geolocation fallback check.
Safety-critical translations are intentionally marked unreviewed.”
