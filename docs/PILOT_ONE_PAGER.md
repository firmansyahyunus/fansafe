# FanSafe Pilot One-Pager

## What FanSafe is

FanSafe is an **offline-first safety companion for international travellers**.
It is a static, privacy-preserving prototype that stores user data locally in
the browser and offers demo city packs, safety phrases, medical-card storage,
trusted-contact check-ins, and scam-risk guidance.

It may also serve as an **open-source supporter-safety reference application
for large international events**.

## What a pilot would test

- Whether travellers can understand the local-only privacy model.
- Whether the safety flows are understandable under time pressure.
- Whether city-pack provenance and sample-data warnings are visible.
- Whether phrase wording is understandable to native speakers.

## Boundaries

FanSafe is not emergency infrastructure, medical advice, a ticket
authentication service, or affiliated with FIFA or a host authority. Current
city data is sample data and must be verified locally. Safety-critical
translations remain unreviewed. No pilot partner, participant, or outcome is
claimed by this document.

## Minimum pilot gate

Before recruiting external travellers, obtain independent human review of
city-pack sources, resolve the denied-geolocation fallback evidence gap, and
agree a participant-consent and incident-escalation plan with the pilot host.
