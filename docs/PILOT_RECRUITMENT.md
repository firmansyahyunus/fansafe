## Outreach message draft

> Subject: Early tester feedback for FanSafe (offline travel-safety app)
>
> Hi — I'm building FanSafe, an offline-first, privacy-preserving safety
> and translation companion for international travellers (not affiliated
> with any government, embassy, or emergency service — full disclaimers in
> the app). It's currently a prototype: no live emergency integration, and
> the demo data covers 4 cities.
>
> I'm looking for 10–30 people to try a short set of realistic scenarios
> (setting it up as if arriving somewhere new, finding an emergency phrase
> under simulated time pressure, checking offline behavior) and tell me
> honestly where it's confusing or where they'd want more before a real
> trip. Sessions are short, no real medical or location data is used
> (a fictional persona is provided), and feedback stays anonymous in any
> public summary.
>
> Would your community be open to a short pilot? Happy to share the app and
> the exact scenarios first so you can see what's being asked before
> committing anyone's time.
