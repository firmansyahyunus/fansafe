# Roadmap

This is not a delivery calendar. It records what has been completed, what is
deliberately not planned, and what requires a future owner decision.

## Phase 0 - Evidence baseline: complete

- Repository audit, validation, and release evidence are recorded in `docs/`
  and `docs/release-evidence/v0.1.0-alpha/`.

## Phase 1 - Credible open-source release: complete

- Public repository, CI, contributor and security documentation, and an MIT
  license are in place.
- Brand and app icons are an explicit exception under `TRADEMARK.md`.

## Phase 2 - Reusable contributor data: complete

- `city-packs/*/pack.json` is source-controlled and generated into the app by
  `tools/sync-city-packs.js`.
- All 17 phrase entries are source-controlled and generated by
  `tools/sync-phrases.js`.
- The MIT license covers the repository's copyrightable content; the
  repository makes no copyright claim over underlying emergency-number facts.
- Phrase provenance is recorded in `phrases/PROVENANCE.md`. Translation
  language-quality status remains `unreviewed` pending independent review.

## Phase 3 - Community feedback: ongoing, not a formal pilot

FanSafe does not currently plan a formal pilot program. Validation will come
from community feedback, issue reports, and future contributions. These
signals must be documented before they are used as evidence. No production
readiness, real-world validation, or impact claim follows from this plan.

## Phase 4 - Builder-program readiness: not started

Identify suitable AI builder, startup, open-source, and AI-credit programs,
then prepare the repository artifacts required for a specific application.
See `docs/BUILDER_PROGRAM_READINESS.md`.

Selecting or applying to a program requires checking its current official
terms and explicit owner approval. No hackathon submission is planned.
